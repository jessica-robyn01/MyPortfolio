Fresh Start Juice

Fresh Start cold-pressed juices are created using a variety of fair-trade products. Fresh Start targets those 65 and up with its heart-healthy and organic ingredients. The clean design brings in the color of the veggies with the lines of the complementary flavor . The unique dieline and wrap-around label, combined with the line, draw the eye around the bottle.
Fresh Start was featured in AIGA Locally Sourced Digital Publication 2022, which showcases student work from the Philadelphia region.
