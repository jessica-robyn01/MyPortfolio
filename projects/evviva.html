Evviva Studio Brand Design

In collaboration with Evviva Studio, a wedding planning business based in West Chester, PA, a freelance design opportunity unfolded. The project revolved around the creation of a classy, clean, and elegant brand identity with a touch of old-time charm. Elements such as a distinctive logo, refined stationery, and additional items like stickers and postcards were meticulously crafted. This endeavor perfectly encapsulated the timeless sophistication that Evviva Studio embodies in its approach to crafting unforgettable wedding experiences.
