Fired. Book Design

Fired is a niche book about ceramics, modern pottery, and ceramicists. The target audience consists of educated and/or well-rounded men and women ages 28-55. Intended for those who either have a background in art, a creative personality, or like to admire art. The combination of shapes through line and images aim to embody the freeform and dynamic qualities of ceramics.
