Anu, Visual Identity

Anu is the name of a Sumerian God which translates to “the heavenly one.” Anu is a gluten-free bakery that focuses on making a serious diet restriction into something cheerful and optimistic. The goal of this branding was to defy the stereotypical bakery logos and form something tasteful, modern, and unique. Anu appeals to any gender from ages 20 through 32 who are trendy and social.
